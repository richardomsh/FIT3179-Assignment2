Data Science Job Salaries Across Countries

Average Salary

The map above illustrates the average salary of data science job across countries. We can see that Russia has the highest average salary of USD $157 500. Followed by USA which has an average salary of USD $145 059. On the other hand, countries like Kenya and Iran has an average salary between USD $4000 and $10000.

From the histogram above, we can analyze that majority of people has a salary between $40 000 - $100 000 USD. Furthermore, the maximum amount of salaries is between $580 000 - $600 000 USD.

Top Job Titles

The Word Cloud above is used to illustrate the top job taken by people across the globe. It is observable that most people has Data Scientist as their job as it the largest. Followed by Data Engineer and Data Analyst. However, the rest of the job titles are also related to these top 3.

Despite Data Scientist being the job with most people, it doesn't have the highest paying salary. Instead, Data Engineering Manager has the highest average salary of $153 000 USD. Moreover, the highest reached paying salary goes to the Reasearch Scientist job which is $450 000 USD followed by Data Science Consultant job which is $412 000 USD.

Highest Paying Job

Above is the jobs with the highest paying salary. We can see that Principal Data Engineer has the highest amount of salary among them all at $600 000 USD. Followed by Reasearch Scientist and Financial Data Analyst. Apart from that, the Data Scientist job which has the highest amount of people in it ranked sixth (6th) in the list.
